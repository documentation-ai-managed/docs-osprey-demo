## Issue

- Getting "Invalid credentials" error
- Can't access Osprey Demo dashboard

## Quick Fix

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check Email and Password">
    Make sure your login details are correct. Password is case-sensitive.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Reset Password">
    Click "Forgot password?" on the login page and follow the link sent to your email.
  </Step>
</Steps>
