---
title: How do I create my first project?
description: Instructions for creating projects in Osprey Demo.
---

## Steps

1. Sign in at `https://osprey-demo.com`.
2. Click the "New Project" button in your dashboard.
3. Enter a name and choose a template (or start blank).
4. Click "Create" to finish setup.

## Tip

Projects help you organize your documentation and collaborate easily.
